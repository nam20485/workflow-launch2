## **Beyond Stateless AI: Architecting Long-Horizon Autonomy**

At **Intel Agency**, we believe the next frontier of Artificial Intelligence is not just "smarter" models, but **reliable** ones.

Current Large Language Models (LLMs) are miraculous but flawed. They suffer from "catastrophic forgetting," "contextual drift," and a lack of auditability. For a chatbot, these are annoyances. For critical infrastructure in Defense, Finance, or Healthcare, they are disqualifying failures.

We are an R\&D-first engineering firm dedicated to solving these specific architectural challenges.
